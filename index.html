About Me

My experiences have shaped my perspective, and my photography is an extension of that perspective. My name is Mony Hin. I was born and raised in a refugee camp in Thailand. I grew up in Philadelphia and graduated from Penn State University. After several years in the corporate world, I quit to pursue my passion. I carry my camera wherever I go. I have been lucky enough to travel the world and shoot different events in many places. Whether it is the raucous energy of a boxing match, the buzzing pulse of a city's landscape, or the essence of a love at a wedding, my photography is all about capturing and sharing my perspective. Images are how we remember the moment, documenting our own personal histories and the inspiration around us.
