About Me

Hi, my name is Mony Hin, I am a photographer based out of my hometown of Philadelphia. I love photographing in all types of different situations from people, pets, cities, mountains, oceans, buildings, you get the point. I carry my camera everywhere I go because it is an extension of how I see the world. My perspective has been shaped by all of my experiences in life. I was born and raised in a refugee camp where my family fled the war in Cambodia. I grew up in Philadelphia, went to school at Penn State and now reside in Montgomery County. After college I entered the corporate world; I hated it. My passion for photography has driven me to learn everyday about this craft.
